About 8Layer

8Layer Technologies, Inc. is a pioneer in providing Open-Source software development and consulting services. With over fifteen (15) years of providing high quality innovations, 8Layer has now developed four main umbrellas of products and services - - Big Data, Cyber Security, Satellite Services, and Special Projects for customized products tailor-fitted to the needs and requirements of the customer.

We are the go-to company in the Philippines for providing cost-effective software development, and IT consultancy. 8Layer augments people and business processes through intelligent and modern technologies.

Established in 2005 by its founder Meric B. Mara, 8Layer started as an Open-Source software development and consultancy company that focused on Autonomic Infrastruction and Telephony solutions. In its inception stage, even as a small start-up company, it has earned the trust and has partnered with the big players in the IT industry to continuously provide better solutions to the Filipinos, specifically the local government units and SMEs.

With the 8Layer team’s technical expertise, coupled with a clear direction from its highly motivated and celebrated management team, the company has catapulted to its present position and direction, staying true to its mantra: Be More. Be Useful. Be of Service.
